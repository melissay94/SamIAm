About Sam I Am

Sam I Am is a recipe generator for the week! The inspiration for the name comes from Sam I Am by Doctor Suess, where Sam wants his friend to try new foods. Hopefully this too will inspire people to try new foods!

How it works

  1. You press the generator button! It will kick up some tasty stuff from the Recipe Puppy API. If you're craving chicken dishes, enter chicken below and everything with chicken will come back!
  2. Once you've found a recipe you want, go ahead and assign it a week day! It'll be added to your list.
  3. Open up the "My List" tab and ta-da! There are all your recipes in a nice grocery list format.
  4. Have fun cooking!
